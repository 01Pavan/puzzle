Puzzle Rules

General Instructions

Follow these rules to play the puzzle:

  1. Read the puzzle instructions carefully.
  2. Study the puzzle layout and pieces before starting.
  3. Work on one peice of the puzzle at a time.
  4. please make sure that every peice of that number refers to that particular number of box
  5. Take breaks if needed to avoid frustration.
  6. Have fun and enjoy the challenge!

Tips

  • Start with the starting pieces to create a good looking logic for that puzzle.
  • Be patient and persistent, solving puzzles takes practice!

Note: The puzzle rules are mandatory to see once and make sure that to give an perfect answer.
